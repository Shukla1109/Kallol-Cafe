Our Story

A Cafe Born to
Bring People Together

Tucked away in Sector 4, Kharghar, Kallol Cafe opened on January 23, 2025, with a mission that goes far beyond food. Inspired by a reading cafe in Switzerland — where customers leave handwritten notes in books for the next reader — cousins Soham and Vinay Chandak set out to recreate that magic in Navi Mumbai.

Every corner is designed to spark real conversation. The reading nook, board games, weekend art sessions — all nudging you gently away from your screen and toward the person sitting right in front of you.

The Experience

More Than a Cafe.
A Way of Being.

At Kallol, the menu is just the beginning. Every corner is designed to help you slow down — to look up from your screen, notice the people around you, and actually enjoy where you are.

Inspired by a Swiss reading cafe where strangers leave notes for strangers they’ll never meet, Kallol is our version of quiet, radical hospitality in the heart of Kharghar.
